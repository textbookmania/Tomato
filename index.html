What is Tomato?

Tomato brings together people together who want to exchange their textbooks. Currently the textbooks are pulled from the University of Hawaii at Manoa, ICS curriculum.

Why use Tomato over the other guys?

Tomato was designed for secure, quick transactions. You can rest knowing that your data is safe. It has a simple, intuitive interface that allows you to quickly browse, or make a transaction and let you move on with your day.
