    <article class="markdown-body entry-content" itemprop="mainContentOfPage"><h1><a id="user-content-what-is-tomato" class="anchor" href="#what-is-tomato" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>What is Tomato?</h1>

<p>Tomato brings together people together who want to exchange their textbooks.  Currently the textbooks are pulled from 
the University of Hawaii at Manoa, ICS curriculum.</p>

<p><a href="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/textbookmania/Tomato/master/doc/tomato-home.PNG" target="_blank"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/textbookmania/Tomato/master/doc/tomato-home.PNG" alt="placeholder image" style="max-width:100%;"></a></p>

<h1><a id="user-content-why-use-tomato-over-the-other-guys" class="anchor" href="#why-use-tomato-over-the-other-guys" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Why use Tomato over the other guys?</h1>

<p>Tomato was designed for secure, quick transactions.  You can rest knowing that your data is safe.  It has a simple, 
intuitive interface that allows you to quickly browse, or make a transaction and let you move on with your day.</p>

<h1><a id="user-content-help" class="anchor" href="#help" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Help</h1>

<p>Users can refer to our <a href="https://github.com/textbookmania/Tomato/wiki/User">User Guide</a>.
Developers can refer to our <a href="https://github.com/textbookmania/Tomato/wiki/Developer">Developer Guide</a>.
For more information contact us <a href="https://github.com/textbookmania/Tomato/wiki/Contact">here</a>.</p>

<h1><a id="user-content-where-is-tomato" class="anchor" href="#where-is-tomato" aria-hidden="true"><span class="octicon octicon-link"></span></a>Where is Tomato</h1>

<p>Tomato can be found <a href="http://tbe-tomato.meteor.com/">here</a>.</p>
